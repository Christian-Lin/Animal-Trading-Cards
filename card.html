Dugong

You can estimate a dugong's age by how many rings they have on their tusks, just like a tree!

  • Scientific Name: Dugong dugon
  • Commonly known as: Sea cow
  • Captive worldwide: Three
  • Conservation status: Vulnerable

Dugongs are long-lived; the oldest recorded specimen reached age 73. Currently listed as vulnerable to extinction, but legally protected in many countries. They reside mostly in warm coastal waters from the western Pacific Ocean to the eastern coast of Africa.
